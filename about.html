Since 2006 Enter Cat Litter Market

Our company is a professional manufacturer specializing in R&D, production and sales of cat litter. We mainly use the most advanced technology from abroad to produce high-quality bentonite cat litter and tofu cat litter. The main features of bentonite cat litter are strong condensing and deodorizing ability, low dust and environmental protection, and saving consumption. The main features of tofu cat litter are super agglomeration, strong deodorization, dust-free and toilet flushing.
